Plant Care Guide

Explore our collection of popular indoor plants and learn how to keep them happy and healthy.

Monstera Deliciosa

Popular for its stunning split leaves, the Monstera is easy to care for and makes a bold statement.

Watering

Water when top 2 inches of soil are dry

Light

Bright, indirect light

Temperature

65-85°F (18-29°C)

Snake Plant

Incredibly hardy and air-purifying, the snake plant can survive in almost any condition.

Watering

Water every 2-3 weeks

Light

Low to bright, indirect light

Temperature

55-85°F (13-29°C)

Peace Lily

Elegant white blooms and glossy leaves make this a favorite. Great air purifier.

Watering

Water when soil feels dry, typically weekly

Light

Low to medium, indirect light

Temperature

65-80°F (18-27°C)

Succulents

Water-storing plants that come in endless varieties. Perfect for sunny windowsills.

Watering

Water every 2-3 weeks, less in winter

Light

Bright, direct light

Temperature

60-80°F (15-27°C)
